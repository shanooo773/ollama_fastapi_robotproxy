## Return and Cancellation Policy
Orders can be cancelled free of charge within 14 days of placing the order, provided the
unit has not yet shipped. Once shipped, returns are subject to a 15% restocking fee and
the customer is responsible for return shipping costs.

## Data and Privacy
NovaFleet AMR units collect anonymized navigation and usage telemetry to improve routing
efficiency. No camera footage is stored beyond 24 hours unless explicitly requested by the
customer for troubleshooting purposes.
