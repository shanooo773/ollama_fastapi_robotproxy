# Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does NovaTech offer installation support?**
Yes. Every hardware purchase includes on-site installation and a two-day operator training
session at the customer's facility.

**Q: What is the warranty period on NovaTech products?**
All hardware comes with a standard 12-month warranty covering parts and labor. Extended
warranties of up to 3 years are available as an add-on.

**Q: Can NovaBot Assembly Arm be customized for a specific product line?**
Yes, NovaTech's engineering team offers custom end-effector design and fine-tuned motion
programs for specific assembly tasks, typically delivered within 4-6 weeks.

**Q: Does NovaTech support integration with existing factory software?**
Yes. NovaTech products support Modbus TCP, OPC-UA, and REST API integration with common
MES/ERP systems.

**Q: What industries does NovaTech serve?**
Primarily electronics assembly, packaged goods, and warehouse/logistics operations.
