# About NovaTech Robotics

NovaTech Robotics was founded in 2021 and specializes in AI-powered automation solutions for
manufacturing and logistics. The company is headquartered in Islamabad, Pakistan, with a satellite
engineering office in Lahore.

NovaTech's mission is to make industrial automation accessible to small and medium-sized
manufacturers by combining affordable robotics hardware with easy-to-deploy AI software.

The company currently has 45 employees across engineering, sales, and customer support, and has
deployed robotic systems in over 30 factories across South Asia and the Middle East.

NovaTech was recognized as a "Rising Star in Industrial AI" at the 2025 Asia Robotics Summit.
