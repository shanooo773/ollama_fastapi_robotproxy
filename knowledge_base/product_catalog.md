# Product Catalog

## NovaBot Assembly Arm
A 6-axis robotic arm designed for precision assembly tasks in electronics and small-parts
manufacturing. Payload capacity is 5kg with repeatability of +/- 0.02mm. Integrates with
existing PLC systems via Modbus TCP.

## NovaSort Vision System
A camera-based sorting system that uses computer vision to classify and sort items on a
conveyor belt at up to 120 items per minute. Includes a pre-trained defect-detection model
that can be fine-tuned on a customer's own product images.

## NovaFleet AMR (Autonomous Mobile Robot)
A warehouse mobile robot for pallet transport, using LIDAR-based SLAM navigation. Maximum
payload is 500kg, battery life is 8 hours of continuous operation, and it charges fully in
90 minutes at a docking station.

## NovaBot Assembly Arm Pricing
Starting at $18,500 per unit, with volume discounts available for orders of 5 or more units.
A 12-month hardware warranty and free firmware updates are included.
